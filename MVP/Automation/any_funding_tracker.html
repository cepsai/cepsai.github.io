02 Three things to tell Claude

Your prompt needs just three elements. Be specific and the tracker will be more useful from day one.

Element 1

Topic & search queries

Describe what you want to track. Name the field, the angle, and any intersections. Claude will turn this into 4–6 daily web searches.

AI and the future of work — grants and fellowships for research on how AI changes labour markets, workforce skills, and economic opportunity.
Element 2

Exclusion rules

Say what to filter out. Think about eligibility, geography, and scope so you don't get noise.

Exclude opportunities restricted to universities only, student scholarships, and US-citizens-only grants. Include anything open to research organisations, NGOs, or policy institutes.
Element 3

Output folder

Tell Claude where on your computer the tracker files should live. It will ask you to confirm access.

Save everything to ~/Documents/grants/ai-work-funding

  1. Create the folder .claude/skills/any-funding-tracker/scripts/ in your project or home directory.
