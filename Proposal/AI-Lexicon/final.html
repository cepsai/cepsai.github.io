Methodology

Analytical framework

This lexicon maps foundational AI governance definitions across the EU AI Act and ten US state laws selected for their comprehensive, cross-sectoral AI governance scope. The EU AI Act serves as the primary benchmark, with US state laws assessed for conceptual convergence and divergence.

Definitions are reproduced verbatim where available, or paraphrased by CEPS analysts where the legislative text requires contextualisation. All paraphrased entries are labelled Summary; verbatim citations are labelled Verbatim.

Selection criteria

US state laws were selected based on: comprehensive cross-sectoral AI governance scope; signed or approved status; and relevance of defined terminology to the EU AI Act's conceptual framework. Sector-specific laws were excluded.
