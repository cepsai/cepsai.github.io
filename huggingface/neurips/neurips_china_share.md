## Key Takeaways

- **China overtook the US in 2025.** China went from 30.0% to 36.4% overall while the US dropped from 38.3% to 32.1%. The flip is most dramatic in posters (37.6% vs 30.7%). The US still leads in Oral + Spotlight (35.8% vs 33.0%), but the gap narrowed from 12pp to under 3pp.
- **Europe slightly declined** across all subsets (16.8% to 15.2% overall).
- **Rest of World grew modestly** from 15.0% to 16.3%.
- Note: "Rest of World" includes institutions explicitly labeled "Rest of the world" plus a small number of unclassified institutions (1.5% of 2024 total, 0.7% of 2025 total).
- Counts are based on institution-level venue assignments from the processed JSON data files.

Source: OpenReview NeurIPS 2024 & 2025 data, processed via `institution_venues_2024_v14.json` and `institution_venues_v14.json`.
