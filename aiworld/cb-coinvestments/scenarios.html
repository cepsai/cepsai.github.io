Check the data: six real funding scenarios

Each card is one real Crunchbase round with a different co-funder configuration. The left chart is the market's capital-flow sankey for that round's year — the highlighted cell is where this round is counted. The right chart is the "who co-invests with whom" syndication sankey for the round's anchor country and year — highlighted links are the partner flows this round contributes to.

To re-derive every number from the raw CSVs independently, run python3 verify_scenarios.py in the cb/fragmentation/ project folder.

What to check by hand: look a company up on Crunchbase, confirm the investor list and round size, then confirm the classification: (1) how many distinct member states / US states do the located investors span? ≥2 → multi-jurisdiction. (2) Is an investor from the company's own jurisdiction present? Yes → invests domestically. (3) Every pair of investor countries in the round adds one joint round to that country pair in the syndication sankey and the country-pair tables.
